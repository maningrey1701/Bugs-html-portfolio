About Bugs

Bugs Bunny is a clever and witty cartoon rabbit known for his laid-back attitude and quick thinking. Created by Warner Bros. in the 1940s, he became the star of the Looney Tunes series and quickly gained popularity with his catchphrase, “What’s up, Doc?” Often seen chewing a carrot, Bugs is famous for outsmarting his enemies—especially Elmer Fudd, Daffy Duck, and Yosemite Sam—with humor and confidence. With his cool personality and timeless charm, Bugs Bunny remains one of the most iconic animated characters in history.
